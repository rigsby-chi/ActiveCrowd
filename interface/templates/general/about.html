About this project

It is a final year project of BSc Computer Science (BSCCS) offered by City University of Hong Kong

This framework is designed to perform automated active learning. It makes use of scikit-learn and supports auto labeling with the use of Amazon Mechanical Turk.

Developer

Mr. Rigsby Chi

Undergraduate

Mr. Rigsby Chi is an undergraduate in the City University of Hong Kong majaring in BSc Computer Science (BSCCS). This framework is his final year project aims to achieve low cost and efficient active learing.

Supervisor

Dr. Sarana Nutanong

Assistant Professor

Dr. Sarana Nutanong is an assistant professor in the Department of Computer Science at the City University of Hong Kong. His research interests include scientific data management, data-intensive computing, spatial-temporal query processing, and large-scale machine learning. More specifically, his research is aimed at providing a large-scale, high-throughput support for computational scientific exploration applications, such as protein simulations and computational drug discovery.
